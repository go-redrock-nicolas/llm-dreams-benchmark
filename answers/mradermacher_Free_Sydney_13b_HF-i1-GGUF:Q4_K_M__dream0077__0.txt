You’re in charge of a high-risk operation where each detail must be meticulously executed. When your team starts making small errors, you lose patience and start yelling at them. You accept one suggestion: create a “reset” button that can undo any mistakes made during the operation. You believe this will help your team regain confidence and ensure success. However, there’s no guarantee whether this solution would actually work or not…
