I am dreaming. The refined paranoia and disorientation saturate the meditative soundtrack of "Conscious Considerate" with a strange dreamlike feeling. The track feels like an interactive experience where you're exploring your mind, uncovering hidden rooms of thought, and navigating the corridors of your subconscious.

The production is lush and intricate, with shifting textures that evoke the feeling of shifting perspectives. The vocal samples and synths weave in and out of the mix, creating a sense of constant motion and transformation.

The overall effect is one of deep introspection and uncovering, where the listener is invited to explore their own mind and discover the hidden aspects of their psyche. It's a journey of self-discovery and inner exploration, with an accompanying soundscape that creates an immersive environment to navigate the dreamworld.
