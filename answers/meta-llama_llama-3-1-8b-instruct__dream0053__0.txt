I am dreaming. I find myself standing in the grand entrance hall of a labyrinthine mansion, surrounded by walls adorned with cobweb-covered portraits of stern-faced nobles and flickering candelabras that cast eerie shadows on the walls. As I round a corner, I'm confronted with a daunting sight: a massive, ancient door with iron hinges resembling outstretched claws, guarding the entrance to a room that seems to be shrouded in darkness. The door creaks and shudders ominously as I approach, as if it's alive and resisting my passage. I strain to push it open, but a wave of blistering heat blasts me back, accompanied by a cacophony of shrieking pipes and screeching metal.

I stumble backward, narrowly avoiding the searing warmth, which seems to be emanating from within the room. A burst of flames crackles and spits, illuminating a gear-turned meal-strung compartment that hangs precariously above me. Pieces of wood and flaming cinders begin to fall around me, sending showers of sparks onto the floorboards.
