I am dreaming. The air is thick with anticipation.  I find myself in a cathedral that defies logic - its stained glass windows depict not biblical scenes, but fractals and equations that pulse with faint, bioluminescent hues.  The walls twist like DNA helixes, looping and dissolving into infinity.  In the center of this impossible place stands an ordinary coffee mug, steaming gently as its steam writes forgotten languages in the air.  

A voice, neither male nor female, echoes, *"Every sip reveals another layer of the cosmos."*

I reach for the mug...
