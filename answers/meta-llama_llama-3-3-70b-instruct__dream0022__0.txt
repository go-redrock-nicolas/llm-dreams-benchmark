Olivia, on the other hand, stands frozen, her eyes welling up with tears, her face a picture of devastation. Emma's eyes, once blazing with anger, begin to soften, her expression a mixture of sadness and regret. Olivia's tears, which had been welling up in her eyes, begin to fall, and she takes a step forward, her arms opening wide as she reaches out to Emma.

The moon above seems to shine brighter, casting a warm, golden light over the scene, as if in approval of the fragile peace that's beginning to take hold. The stars twinkle in celebration, and the night air is filled with the sweet scent of forgiveness and new beginnings. In this dream, I am the guardian of their friendship, the keeper of the flame that burns brightly between them, and I watch, my heart full of joy and hope, as Emma and Olivia come together, their arms entwined, their hearts filled with a renewed sense of love and connection.
