As the dream begins to fade, I'm left with a haunting question: what will I do with this power? Will I use it to build a kingdom of influence, to bend the world to my will? The dream is lost, but the question remains, a tantalizing enigma that haunts me like a specter, a reminder that even in the depths of my own imagination, there are consequences to playing god.
