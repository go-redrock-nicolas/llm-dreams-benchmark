The imposter's actions, once a source of pain and confusion, now seem like a fascinating puzzle to be solved.

The cliff begins to fade, and I find myself back in the library, the book still open in my hands. I close the book, feeling a sense of wonder and curiosity, and the library dissolves around me, leaving me suspended in a state of dreamy contemplation.
