Across from her, the enigmatic Kaelin, a radical thinker with a heart full of empathy, gazes out at the gathering with an intensity that borders on the fierce. As the discussion deepens, the crystal spires begin to glow with an intense, pulsing light, casting an otherworldly glow across the gathering. The air is charged with a sense of wonder, as if the very essence of the conversation is being distilled into a potent elixir of empathy and understanding.
