I am dreaming. You enter a realm where your conscious intentions instantly reshape your surroundings, from landscape to physical laws. As you explore the immediate consequences of your thoughts, you notice that some of them have unexpected results. You wonder if there's someone observing this and manipulating it. You decide to investigate and see what happens next.

You create a portal between dream world and reality using an ancient ritual engraved on a stone tablet. As you chant the words and perform the gestures, energy flows around you, forming a vortex that connects both realms.

Suddenly, darkness fills the room as you cross over to the other side. You come out of the portal in an alley at night, surrounded by tall buildings with neon lights flickering on their facades. You look around cautiously and wonder where do you go from here...
