The world above fades away as I descend deeper and deeper, the light dimming until I am surrounded by a serene, otherworldly darkness. My fingers brush against the smooth surface of the box, and I feel a surge of energy course through me, as if the very essence of the universe is flowing into my being.

Instead, I am filled with a newfound sense of empowerment and clarity. I rise to the surface of the lake, breaking through the shimmering waters with a triumphant exhale. The world above is now a place of endless possibilities, where the truth sets me free and allows me to embrace my true self.
